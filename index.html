Kelly Greer

In The Beginning

Born in Fresno, California on August 24th, 1989, I have spent the majority of my life dedicated to Art, Skateboarding and Music. After graduating for Bullard High School in 2007, I went to Fresno City College and began working out at the gym until 2009.

Military Life

In November 2010 after partcipating in round 2 of the Guitar Center national "drum off" competition, I decided to enlist in the US Army. In March 2010, I attended Basic Training at Fort Benning, Georgia. I attended Advanced Individual "MOS" Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia as a 25L Cable Installer Maintainer, or Fiber Optic Technician. After Graduating from AIT, I deployed to K-16 Air Base in South Korea until October 2011. From 2011 until my ETS date, I served in the 16 TIN Signal Company, 357th Battalion, 11th Signal Brigade at Fort Hood, Texas.

Future Goals

My future goals are to achieve my maximum potential physically and mentally, so that I can maximize my education in specialty skills and work in the tech industry. My ultimate goal would be to works towards the fight against climate change
